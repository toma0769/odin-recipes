Fillet Steak

A top-tier fried cut of fillet steak

Ingredients

  • Fillet Steak
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Begin with drying the steak with a paper towel
  2. Add some olive oil to the pan on a high heat
  3. While the olive oil heats up, add some salt generously to the steak
  4. Throw the steak in the frying pan. It should sizzle!
  5. Cook for 6-8 mins for rare, 8-10 mins for medium, and 10-12 mins for well done
  6. Leave the steak to stand for 5 mins
  7. Enjoy!
