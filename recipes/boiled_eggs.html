Boiled Eggs

How to cook the perfect soft/hard boiled eggs.

Ingredients

  • Medium Sized Eggs
  • Water

Steps

  1. Firstly, add the eggs to a decent-sized pan
  2. Add water, covering the eggs with an extra inch or two
  3. Bring the water to the boil
  4. Simmer for 4 mins for a soft boiled egg, 6-7 mins for a hard boiled egg
  5. Cool your eggs down in some cold water
  6. Enjoy!
